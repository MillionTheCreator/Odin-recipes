    Pelmeni - Traditional Russian Dumplings

    Pelmeni Dough:

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/2 cup water

    Instructions:

    Prepare the Dough:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
    • Make a well in the center and add the egg and water.
    • Mix to form a dough, then knead until smooth.
    • Wrap in plastic and let it rest for 30 minutes.

    Pelmeni Filling:

    Ingredients:

    • 1/2 pound ground beef
    • 1/2 pound ground pork
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions:

    Prepare the Filling:
    • Mix beef, pork, onion, and garlic in a bowl.
    • Season with salt and pepper; mix well.

    Assemble and Cook:

    Instructions:

    Assemble the Pelmeni:
    • Roll out dough and cut small circles.
    • Place a small amount of filling in each circle.
    • Fold and seal to create dumplings.
    Cook the Pelmeni:
    • Boil water and add salt.
    • Drop pelmeni into boiling water.
    • Cook until they float to the surface.
    • Remove with a slotted spoon and serve.

    Serving Suggestions

    Pelmeni can be served with sour cream, butter, or vinegar.
